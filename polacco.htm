Margo, Patricia Polacco, Melanie
November 1999
Patricia Polacco visited our school's Media Center. She is the author of children's books and is almost as famous as our school's Media Specialist :-) !

Ms. Polacco told us about her books, her life, and taught us that no one likes to be teased. She let us touch an actual meteor! And she signed copies of her books for us.
